The Smart Spirulina System was developed by Jimmy Tang and Regina Flores Mir as part of their Research Fellowship at Terreform One during the summer of 2015.




Jimmy is a New York based multidisciplinary designer. He has a background in visual design and web development. He focuses on designing for social innovation through interactive media and creative technologies. He has a strong interest in edible design, biotech, citizen science and the maker culture. He holds a Master of Fine Art degree in Design and Technology from Parsons School of Design.



Regina is a New York based creative technologist who is particularly fond of using code as a medium of expression. She has a diverse background in atrophysics, finance and consulting. She has interests in algorithmic art and bio design. She holds a BA in Physics, an MA in Statistics and will soon hold an MFA in Design and Technology from Parsons School of Design.
